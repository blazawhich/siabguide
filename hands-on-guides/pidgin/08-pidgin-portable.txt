# Portable Pidgin and OTR for Windows

## Differences between the Installed and Portable Versions of Pidgin

Given that portable tools are *not* installed on a local computer, their existence and use will remain undetected. However, keep in mind that your external device or USB memory stick and portable tools are only as safe as the computer you are using, and may risk being exposed to adware, malware, spyware and viruses. 

There are no other differences between **Portable Pidgin** and the version designed to be installed on a local computer.

## Download and Extract Portable Pidgin

To begin downloading and extracting **Portable Pidgin**, perform the following steps:

### Step 1

**Click** [**http://portableapps.com/apps/internet/pidgin_portable**](http://portableapps.com/apps/internet/pidgin_portable) to be directed to the appropriate download site.

### Step 2

**Click** ![Download Now](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/01.png)[Pidgin img 61] to activate its associated **Source Forge** download page.

### Step 3

**Click** ![Save File](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/02.png)[Pidgin img 62] to save the ![installer](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/03.png)[Pidgin img 63] installation file to your computer; then **navigate** to it.

### Step 4

**Double click** ![installer](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/03.png)[Pidgin img 63]; the *Open File - Security Warning* dialog box may appear. If it does, **click** ![Run](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/04.png)[Pidgin img 64] to activate the following screen:

![Language Installer window](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/05.png)[Pidgin img 65]

*Figure 1: The Language Installer window*

### Step 5

**Click** ![OK](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/06.png)[Pidgin img 66] to activate the following screen:

![Pidgin Portable | PortableApps.com window](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/07.png)[Pidgin img 67]

*Figure 2: The Pidgin Portable | PortableApps.com window*

### Step 6

**Click** ![Next](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/08.png)[Pidgin img 68] to activate the following screen:

![Choose Components](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/09.png)[Pidgin img 69]

*Figure 3: The Choose Components window*

**Note**: **Click** to enable the ![Additional Languages](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/10.png)[Pidgin img 70] option, and include multilingual support if you would prefer to use **Portable Pidgin** in a language other than English. Enabling this option will make the extraction process a little bit longer. 

### Step 7

**Click** ![Next](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/08.png)[Pidgin img 68] to activate the *Choose Install Location* window, and then **click** ![Browse](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/11.png)[Pidgin img 71] to activate the following screen:

![Browse for Folder](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/12.png)[Pidgin img 72]

*Figure 4: The Browse for Folder window*

### Step 8

**Navigate** to the destination external hard drive or USB memory stick, **select** it and then **click** ![OK](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/13.png)[Pidgin img 73] to confirm its location, and to return to the *Choose Install Location* window. 

### Step 9

**Click** ![Install](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/14.png)[Pidgin img 74] to begin extracting **Portable Pidgin** to the specified folder; then **click** ![Finish](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/15.png)[Pidgin img 75] to complete the installation process.

### Step 10

**Navigate** to your destination external drive or USB memory stick, as shown in *Figure 5* below, and then **open** it to confirm that the **Portable Pidgin** program was successfully extracted.

![Browse for Folder](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/pidginportable-en-1/16.png)[Pidgin img 76]

*Figure 5: The Browse for Folder window*

Before you may begin using **Portable Pidgin** in a safe and secure manner, you must first download and extract its complementary portable **Off-the-Record** (**OTR**) plugin.
