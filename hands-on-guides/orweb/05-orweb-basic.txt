# Start Orweb

## Step 1

To open Orweb you **tap** the application's icon.

![application's icon](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/orweb.png)[Orweb img 06]

## Step 2

**Orweb** will launch and automatically try to connect to *https://check.torproject.org*, to ensure that its connection to the **Tor** network is working.  If it can connect, you will see a message telling you that your *browser is configured to use Tor*.  If **Orweb** can not connect to the website you will see an error message in the browser. If this happens, it is suggested that you check that [Orbot](https://securityinabox.org/Orbot_main)[Orbot Main] is installed and running on your android device if this happens.

![Orweb successfully connected](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/006.png)[Orweb img 07] ![Orweb connection failure](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/007.png)[Orweb img 08]

*Figure 1 & 2: Orweb successfully connected and Orweb connection failure screens*

## Step 3

To browse to websites, **tap** the area at the top of the screen to the right of ![](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/008.png)[Orweb img 09] and type in the address you want to visit. Press **Go** on the onscreen keypad.

![Browsing to a new website](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/009.png)[Orweb img 10] ![](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/010.png)[Orweb img 11]

*Figure 3 & 4: Browsing to a new website

# Change your UserAgent

If you want to hide the type of device that you are using from the websites you visit, **Orweb** can be configured to pretend to be a number of different devices.

## Step 1

**Tap** on the menu icon (![menu icon](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/011.png)[Orweb img 12]) found in the top right of the screen and **tap** ![Settings](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/012.png)[Orweb img 13].

![Settings Menu](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/013.png)[Orweb img 14]

*Figure 5: Settings Menu*

## Step 2

Once in the *settings* section, scroll down to the **Privacy** section and **tap** ![User Agent](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/014.png)[Orweb img 15]

![User Agent option](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/015.png)[Orweb img 16]

*Figure 6: User Agent option screen*

## Step 3

You will be presented with a list of *User Agents*. **Tap** your choice (e.g. Tap *iPhone*  to set it.  Now any time you visit a website, it will think you are using an *iPhone*).

![A list of possible User Agents](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/016.png)[Orweb img 17]

*Figure 7: A list of possible User Agents*

# Clear your browser history

## Step 1

To manually clear your browsing history and cache, and to hide the websites you have been visiting on your phone, **tap** on the menu icon (![menu icon](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/011.png)[Orweb img 12]) and press ![Clear History/Cache](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/017.png)[Orweb img 18]

## Step 2

To automatically remove your browsing history and cache as you go to a new page, **Tap** on the menu icon (![menu icon](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/011.png)[Orweb img 12]) followed by ![Settings](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/012.png)[Orweb img 13]

## Step 3

In the **settings** screen, scroll down and **tap** *Clear Back History*.

![Setting the Clear Back History option](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/018.png)[Orweb img 19]

*Figure 8: Setting the Clear Back History option*

Note: When you set this, you will not be able to press the back button to view web pages you have already visited.

# Clear your cookies

**Note:** Deleting cookies will sign you out of any websites that you were logged into.

## Step 1

**Tap** on the menu icon (![menu icon](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/011.png)[Orweb img 12]) found in the top right of the screen and **tap** **Settings**

## Step 2

Scroll down to the **Cookies** section and press ![Clear Cookie Data](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/019.png)[Orweb img 20]

## Step 3

**Tap** ![OK](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/020.png)[Orweb img 21] to confirm the deleting of cookies.

![Confirm deletion of cookies](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orweb-en-1/021.png)[Orweb img 22]

*Figure 9: Confirm deletion of cookies*
