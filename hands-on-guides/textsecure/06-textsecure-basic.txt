# Establish secure communication

It is required to do a one-time secure connection per phone number with which you like to use **TextSecure**. To do this:

## Step 1

Go to the *Menu*, and **click** on *secure session* 

![Menu options](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-09.png)

*Figure 8: Menu options*

## Step 2

**Enter** or **select** the desired contact to **Initiate Key Exchange**

![Secure session initiation](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-10.png)     ![Secure session initiation](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-1011.png)

*Figures 9 and 10: Secure session initiation*

## Step 3

**Press** *send*.

Your **TextSecure** app will send a message to the recipient, whose **TextSecure** app will **AUTOMATICALLY** respond with a message to establish a **secure connection**. This process must be carried out once for each phone number or contact.

![Key exchange messages](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-11.png)     ![Key exchange messages](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-12.png)

*Figures 11 and 12: Key exchange messages*

## Step 4

When the secure connection with this contact is established, a locked padlock icon will appear on the top-left corner.

![Key exchange message sent](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-13.png)

*Figure 12: Key exchange message sent*

 **Note**: You can change the settings to prevent **TextSecure** from replying automatically by **choosing** *Menu*, and then *Settings*

![Menu options](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-14.png)

*Figure 13: Menu options*

## Step 5

**Scroll** down to *Complete Key Exchanges*. This option will automatically complete key exchanges for new secure sessions or for existing sessions with the same identity key.

![Settings](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-15.png)

*Figure 14: Settings*

## Step 5

**Uncheck** the box to turn this option off as it appears below.

![omplete Key Exchanges unchecked](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-16.png)

*Figure 15: Complete Key Exchanges unchecked*

# Verify the identity of your contact

To verify that connection you established is with the right person, you can follow these steps.

## Step 1

**Select** the text message that was automatically sent by **TextSecure** to establish the secure connection.

![Sample selected text message](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-17.png)

*Figure 16: Sample selected text message*

## Step 2

**Select** *Menu* and then **tap** *Secure Session Options* to activate the following screen:

![Menu options](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-18.png)

*Figure 17: Menu options*

## Step 3

**Tap** *Verify Recipient Identity*. This will display a set of characters for you as well as the person on the other side.

![Secure session options](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-19.png)
 
*Figure 18: Secure session options*

## Step 4

Contact the other person (via phone or other secure channels) and confirm they see the same set of characters.

# Exchange encrypted messages

## Step 1

**Tap** the **TextSecure** icon.

## Step 2

**Compose** a new message.

![Home screen](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-20.png)

*Figure 19: Home screen*

## Step 3

**Select** the desired contact.

![Contact selection field](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-21.png)

*Figure 20: Contact selection field*

## Step 4

**Verify** the padlock appears in the send button to signify that your text will be secure.

![Message composition field](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-2213.png)

*Figure 21: Message composition field*

## Step 5

**Write** the message.

![Sample message](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/textsecure-and-2313.png)

*Figure 22: Sample message*

## Step 6

**Click** *Send*.

**Important:** If the padlock doesn't appear next to the send button as you are writing your text message, it means that the message you will be sending is travelling in plain-text, and is can be intercepted and recorded along the way.
