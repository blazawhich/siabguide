# Install ObscuraCam

## Step 1

**Download** the app from the [Google play store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.witness.sscphase1&hl=en)

![ObscuraCam in the Google Play store](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/obscuracam-and-02.png)

*Figure 1: ObscuraCam in the Google Play store*

## Step 2

**Confirm** the permissions required by the app and begin installation by tapping the *Install* button.

![Confirmation of permissions and installation process](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/obscuracam-and-03.png) ![Confirmation of permissions and installation process](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/obscuracam-and-04.png)
![Confirmation of permissions and installation process](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/obscuracam-and-05.png)

*Figures 3, 4, and 5: Confirmation of permissions and installation process*

## Step 3

**Tap** *Open* to run the app for the first time.

** Step 4

**Read** the *Terms of Use* carefully. You can **accept** by tapping *I Accept*.

![Terms of Use](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/obscuracam-and-06.png)

*Figure 6: Terms of Use*
