# Customised Settings

Once avast! is installed and running on your computer, the following settings can be configured as required.

## SSL Scanning of Email Connections

By default, **avast!** *Mail Shield* tool will scan all email including SSL/TLS encrypted connections. This can can cause issues for some email clients such as **Thunderbird**. SSL scanning of email connections can be disabled to prevent **avast!** interfering with email clients.

### Step 1

**Click** ![Settings](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/18.png) from the left hand column of the !avast home screen

### Step 2

**Click** ![Active Protection](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/29.png) and **select** *Mail Shield* and *Customize* as shown below.

![Mail Shield - Customise](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/30.png)

*Figure 14: Mail Shield - Customise*

### Step 3

**Click** ![SSL scanning](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/31.png) and **unselect** *SSL Scanning* as shown below.

![SSL Scanning option](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/32.png)

*Figure 15: SSL Scanning option*

## Disable voiceover sounds

By default, **avast!** will enable 'Voiceover' sounds where available. This can be disabled by following the steps below.

### Step 1

**Click** ![Settings](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/18.png) from the left hand column of the !avast home screen

### Step 2

**Click** ![General](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/33.png) and **select** the *Sound* settings arrow as highlighted below.

![Sound settings](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/34.png)

*Figure 16: Sound settings*

### Step 3

**Unselect** *Use voiceover sounds (where available)* as shown above.
