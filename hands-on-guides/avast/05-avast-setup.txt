# Configure avast! (optional)

We recommend changing a few of Avast's default settings. The steps below will help you: 

* Reduce the amount of information that your computer sends to the company that makes *avast!*
* Disable a handful of unusual features that go beyond core anti-malware functionality
* Enable a few additional *avast!* privacy features

### Step 1

![Avast img 50](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.066.png)[Avast img 50]

1. Click the *Settings* tab

### Step 2

![Avast img 51](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.067.png)[Avast img 51]

1. Uncheck the box next to *Participate in the Avast community*
2. Click *OK*

### Step 3

By default, Avast's *Mail Shield* will scan all email, including messages sent or received through an encrypted connection. This can can cause issues for mail clients like **Thunderbird**. The following steps will help you disable the scanning of encrypted email connections to prevent **avast!** from interfering with your mail client.

![Avast img 52](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.068.png)[Avast img 52]

1. Click *Settings*

### Step 4

![Avast img 53](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.069.png)[Avast img 53]

1. Click *Active protection*
2. Click the *Customize* link next to *Mail Shield*

### Step 5

![Avast img 54](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.070.png)[Avast img 54]

1. Click *SSL Scanning*
2. **Uncheck** the box next to *Scan SSL Connections*

### Step 6

The following Step will prevent *avast!* from adding text to your outgoing emails.

![Avast img 55](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.072.png)[Avast img 55]

1. Click *Behaviour*
2. **Uncheck** the box next to *Insert note into clean message (outgoing)*
3. Click *OK*

## Configure the Avast extension for Mozilla Firefox

If you had the *Mozilla Firefox* browser on your computer when you installed *avast!*, the installer will have loaded a browser-specific extension. The following steps will help you configure that extension and improve its privacy.

### Step 1 (Firefox)

![Avast img 56](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.034.png)[Avast img 56]

* Launch *Mozilla Firefox*
1. Check the *Allow this installation* box
2. Click *Continue*

### Step 2 (Firefox)

![Avast img 57](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.035.png)[Avast img 57]

1. The *avast!* extension may be disabled on your browser's homepage. In order to configure it, you must visit an actual website

### Step 3 (Firefox)

![Avast img 58](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.036.png)[Avast img 58]

1. Go to any website.
2. Click the *avast!* button
3. Click *Settings*

### Step 4 (Firefox)

![Avast img 59](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.037.png)[Avast img 59]

1. Check the box next to *Do not track*
* Scroll down, if necessary, to see additional configuration options

### Step 5 (Firefox)

![Avast img 60](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.038.png)[Avast img 60]

1. **Uncheck** the box next to *SiteCorrect*
2. **Uncheck** the box next to *Statistics*
3. **Uncheck** the box next to *Search engine results*
4. **Uncheck** the box next to *SafePrice*
5. Click *Save*

### Step 6 (Firefox)

![Avast img 61](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.039.png)[Avast img 61]

1. Click the *X* in the upper, right-hand corner of the *avast!* window.
