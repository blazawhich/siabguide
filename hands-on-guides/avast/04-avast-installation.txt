# How to Install and Register avast!

## How to Install avast!

Installing **avast!** is a relatively easy and quick procedure. To begin installing **avast!**, perform the following steps:

#### Step 1

**Double click** ![](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-15.png); the *Open File - Security Warning* dialog box may appear. If it does, **click** *Yes to activate avast! Installation as follows*:

![Installation screen](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/03.png)

*Figure 1: The Free Antivirus Setup-avast! Installation screen*

#### Step 2

**Unselect** *Yes, install Dropbox* as shown in *figure 1* and **click** ![Custom Installation](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/04.png).

Note: Selecting *Custom installation* will allow you to change language settings for your avast!. Otherwise you are able to use *Regular installation*.

#### Step 3

Accept the default installation folder and **click** ![Continue](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/05.png) to activate the following dialog box:

![Which components do you want to install?](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/06.png)

*Figure 2: The 'Which components do you want to install?' window*

#### Step 4

**Select** a language, accept all other defaults and **click** ![Continue](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/05.png)

#### Step 5

After reading the license agreement **click** ![Continue](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/05.png) at the *Please Do Not Skip - Read it Carefully* screen to begin the installation. The *opt-out* option will be discussed later in this chapter.

![Install the product window](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/07.png)

*Figure 3: Install the product window*

#### Step 6

**Click** ![Done](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/08.png) to complete the installation. avast! will launch a quick scan of your computer as shown below:

![!avast quick scan](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/09.png)

*Figure 4: !avast quick scan*

*Note* Refer to section [4.7 How to Deal with Viruses](https://securityinabox.org/dealingwithviruses#4.7) if !avast detects infected files during the quick scan shown in *figure 4*

Once the quick scan has completed, the **!avast** homepage will display as shown in the screen below. You have now completed the install of **!avast**

![Thank you for installing !avast](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/10.png)

*Figure 5: Thank you for installing !avast Free Antivirus*

**Important** You *must* register your copy of the software, to ensure that the software engines, virus definitions and the program itself are updated on a regular basis. This is described in the next section.

## How to Register avast!

**Note**: If you do not register your copy of **avast!** it will stop working after 30 days. You must be connected to the Internet to register **avast!**.

To register your copy of **avast!** perform the following steps:

#### Step 1

**Click** ![Register](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/11.png) at the *figure 5* above to activate the following screen:

![Select your level of protection](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/12.png)

*Figure 9: Select your level of protection*

#### Step 2

**Click** ![Select](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/13.png) under the *Standard protection* column as highlighted above in *figure 9*

#### Step 3

At the *Register your !avast Free Antivirus* enter your email address as shown in following screen and **click** ![Register with email address](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/15.png)

![Register your !avast Free Antivirus screen](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/14.png)

*Figure 10: The Register your !avast Free Antivirus screen*

**Note***: Your *email* is the only *mandatory* text field.

#### Step 4

**Click** *Stay with basic protection* as highlighted in the following screen:

![Thank you for registering with avast!](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/16.png)

*Figure 11: The Thank you for registering with avast! screen*

You have now completed registering of your copy of **avast!**

![You Are Protected](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/17.png)

*Figure 12: You Are Protected screen*

### *Opt-Out* for the avast! Community

During the avast! installation process, avast! will automatically enable the *Participate in the avast! community* option. For reasons of internet privacy and security, it is recommended that you disable this option as shown in the following steps:

#### Step 1

**Click** ![Settings](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/18.png) from the left hand column of the avast! home screen

#### Step 2

From the *General* tab **unselect** the *Participate in the Avast community* as shown in the following screen:

![The Participate in the Avast community screen[(https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/avast-en-1/19.png)

*Figure 13: The Participate in the Avast community screen*
