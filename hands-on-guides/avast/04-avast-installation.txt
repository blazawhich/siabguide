# Download and install avast!

## Step 1

![Avast img 01](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.001.png)[Avast img 01]

1. Go to the [avast! website](http://www.avast.com/index)
2. Click on *Go to Download*

## Step 2

![Avast img 02](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.002.png)[Avast img 02]

1. Click *Free Download*

## Step 3

![Avast img 03](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.003.png)[Avast img 03]

1. Click *Save File*

## Step 4

![Avast img 04](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.004.png)[Avast img 04]

1. Go to your *Downloads* folder (or wherever you put the avast! installer)
2. Double click the *avast_free_antivirus_setup_online* file

## Step 5

![Avast img 05](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.005.png)[Avast img 05]

1. Click *Yes* to install avast!

## Step 6

![Avast img 06](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.006.png)[Avast img 06]

The *avast!* installer should appear

1. **Uncheck** the *Yes, install Dropbox...* checkbox box
2. Click *Regular installation*

## Step 7

![Avast img 07](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.007.png)[Avast img 07]

1. We will walk through the process of "opting out" from this data collection after we install *avast!*
2. Click *Continue*

## Step 8

![Avast img 08](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.008_0.png)[Avast img 08]

1. The progress bar will track the installation of *avast!*

## Step 9

![Avast img 09](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.009.png)[Avast img 09]

1. When the installation is complete, click *Done*

## Step 10

![Avast img 10](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.010.png)[Avast img 10]

1. After the installation completes, click *Skip this tutorial*

![Avast img 15](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.028.png)[Avast img 15]

![Avast img 16](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.029.png)[Avast img 16]

![Avast img 17](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.030.png)[Avast img 17]

1. Click the contents of the *Language* select box to choose a different language

![Avast img 18](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.031.png)[Avast img 18]

# Register avast! if you do not yet have an account

## Step 1

![Avast img 19](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.011_0.png)[Avast img 19]

1. Click the *Settings* tab

## Step 2

![Avast img 20](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.014.png)[Avast img 20]

1. Click the *Registration* tab
2. Note that you will only be able to use *avast!* for 30 days unless you register. Registration, which is free, allows you to use the tool for a full year. After one year, you will have to renew your registration.
3. Click *Register now*

## Step 3

![Avast img 21](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.015.png)[Avast img 21]

1. Click the grey *Select* button under the *Standard protection* column

## Step 4

![Avast img 22](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.016.png)[Avast img 22]

1. Enter values for *First name* and *Last name*. (You do not, of course, have to use your real name.)
2. Enter a *working* email address . (You will have to visit an account-activation link that will be sent to you at this address.)
3. Click *Register with email address*

## Step 5

![Avast img 23](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.017.png)[Avast img 23]

1. Click *Stay with basic protection*

## Step 6

![Avast img 24](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.018.png)[Avast img 24]

1. *avast!* is now registered.
2. Note that you now have a full year before you must renew your registration
3. Click *OK*

## Step 7

![Avast img 25](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.019.png)[Avast img 25]

Sign in to the email account with which you registered *avast!*

## Step 8

![Avast img 26](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.020.png)[Avast img 26]

1. You should find an email from *avast!* in your inbox.
* **Before you click on a link that was sent to you by email, you should always scan the link address and look for anything suspicious. In this case, it is an *HTTPS* link that points to a site within the *avast.com* domain, so it's probably OK. (But you should still double check the URL in your browser, once you get there.)**
2. Click the account verification link.

## Step 9

![Avast img 27](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.021.png)[Avast img 27]

* **Having just followed a link that was sent to you by email, you should scan the website's address for anything suspicious before you create and enter your new *avast!* password.**
1. Once again, this is an *HTTPS* website within the *avast.com* domain—as expected—so it's probably OK
2. Create a strong password for your *avast!* account. Enter it in both fields
3. The green password strength indicator will tell you if you have chosen a good password
* If you use a secure password manager, such as *KeePassX*, now would be a good time to create an entry for this account and save it. 
4. Click *Set password*

## Step 10

![Avast img 28](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.022.png)[Avast img 28]

1. If your are prompted to "Remember" your password, you should select *Never Remember Password for This Site,* unless you have chosen to trust your browser's built-in password manager.
2. Click *Continue*

## Step 11

![Avast img 29](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.024.png)[Avast img 29]

1. Your email address should now be visible in the *Overview* tab of *avast!*

# Register avast! if you already have an account

## Step 1

![Avast img 30](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.032.png)[Avast img 30]

1. Click on the *Account* tab
2. Click the *Sign in* link in the lower, left-hand corner

## Step 2

![Avast img 31](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.033.png)[Avast img 31]

1. Enter the email address you used to create your *avast!* account
2. Enter your *avast!* password
3. Click the blue *Sign in* button

## Step 3

![Avast img 32](https://siabnext.ttc.io/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/avast-win-en.034_0.png)[Avast img 32]

1. Your email address should now be visible on the *Account* tab of *avast!*
