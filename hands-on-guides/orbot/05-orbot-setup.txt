# Configure Orbot

## Step 1

To open Orbot you **tap** the application's icon.

![application's icon](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/orbot.png)[Orbot img 05]

## Step 2

**Tap** the language that you want and then ![Next](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/006.png)[Orbot img 06].

![Choose language](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/005.png)[Orbot img 07]

*Figure 3: Choose language*

## Step 3

A configuration wizard will appear with a description of Orbot. **Read** it and **tap** ![Next](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/006.png)[Orbot img 06] 

![Information about Orbot](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/007.png)[Orbot img 08]

*Figure 4: Information about Orbot*

## Step 4

You will then be presented with a warning screen. Once you have **read** this and understand the requirements press ![Next](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/006.png)[Orbot img 06]

![Important warning about how to use Orbot](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/008.png)[Orbot img 09]

*Figure 5: Important warning about how to use Orbot*

## Step 5

A screen will appear asking you to grant **Orbot** *Superuser access*. *Superuser access* requires that your device is [rooted](https://securityinabox.org/glossary#rooting)[Glossary #rooting] if you want to make use of **Orbot's** Transparent Proxy features - we will not explore this feature in the **Orbot** guide.

If your smartphone is not rooted, just **tick** the option that states *I understand and would like to continue without Superuser*. The next screen explains in order for you to benefit from Tor, you will need to use apps built to work with **Orbot**, or that support HTTP or SOCKS proxy.

![Notes regarding rooting](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/009.png)[Orbot img 10] ![Notes regarding rooting](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/010.png)[Orbot img 11]

*Figure 6 & 7: Notes regarding rooting*

**Note:** If you would like to learn more about *rooting* see the [*Advanced Smartphone Security*](https://securityinabox.org/chapter_11_7)[Smartphones Advanced] chapter.

## Step 6

A list of applications that work with **Orbot** will appear. Take note of them and **tap** ![Next](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/006.png)[Orbot img 06]

![Orbot-enabled apps](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/011.png)[Orbot img 12]

*Figure 8: Orbot-enabled apps*

## Step 7

**Orbot** configuration is now complete. A final screen describing the typical users of **Tor** and **Orbot** will appear, once you have read this **tap** ![Finish](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/012.png)[Orbot img 13]

![Orbot is ready](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/013.png)[Orbot img 14]

*Figure 9: Orbot is ready!*

## Step 8

After pressing ![Finish](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/012.png)[Orbot img 13] (*Fig. 9*) you will be presented with the deactivated **Orbot** screen.

![Orbot deactivated](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/orbot-en-1/014.png)[Orbot img 15]

*Figure 10: Orbot deactivated*
