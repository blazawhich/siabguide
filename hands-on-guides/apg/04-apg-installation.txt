# Install APG

## Step 1

Download** the app from the [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.thialfihar.android.apg). Alternatively, you can download the app from the [project's homepage](http://www.thialfihar.org/projects/apg/).

## Step 2

**Install** the app by tapping the **Install** button.

## Step 3

**Confirm** the permissions required by the app and tap **Accept & download**.

## Step 4

**Tap** *Open* to run the app for the first time.

There are no further initial steps needed for file encryption with a password. If you want to use **AGP in combination with the k9 email client**, please go to the [**K9+APG Hands on Guide**](/en/k9_apg_main).
