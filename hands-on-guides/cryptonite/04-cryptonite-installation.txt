# Install Cryptonite

## Step 1

**Download** the app from the [Google play store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=csh.cryptonite)

![Cryptonite in the Google Play store](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/cryptonite-and-01.png)

*Figure 1: Cryptonite in the Google Play store*

## Step 2

**Read** carefully the permissions required by the app and install it by tapping the **Install** button.


![Permissions required and installation](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/cryptonite-and-02.png) ![](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/cryptonite-and-03.png)

*Figure 2 and 3: Permissions required and installation*

## Step 3

**Tap** *Open* to run the app for the first time.

![App installed](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/cryptonite-and-04.png)

*Figure 4: App installed*

## Step 4

A message will appear as shown below, **read** it carefully and **click** on *I understand*.

![Warning concerning development status](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/cryptonite-and-05.png) 

*Figure 5: Warning concerning development status*

## Step 5

In the *Preferences* tab, make sure *Use external storage* is *not* activated. 

![Preferences](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/cryptonite-and-27.png) ![](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/cryptonite-and-28.png)

*Figure 6 and 7: Preferences*
