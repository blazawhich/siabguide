# Install Cryptonite

## Step 1

**Download** the app from the [Google play store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=csh.cryptonite)

![Cryptonite in the Google Play store](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/cryptonite-en/01.png)[Cryptonite img 01]

*Figure 1: Cryptonite in the Google Play store*

## Step 2

**Read** carefully the permissions required by the app and install it by tapping the **Install** button.


![Permissions required](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/cryptonite-en/02.png)[Cryptonite img 02] ![Installation](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/cryptonite-en/03.png)[Cryptonite img 03]

*Figure 2 and 3: Permissions required and installation*

## Step 3

**Tap** *Open* to run the app for the first time.

![App installed](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/cryptonite-en/04.png)[Cryptonite img 04]

*Figure 4: App installed*

## Step 4

A message will appear as shown below, **read** it carefully and **click** on *I understand*.

![Warning concerning development status](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/cryptonite-en/05.png)[Cryptonite img 05]

*Figure 5: Warning concerning development status*

## Step 5

In the *Preferences* tab, make sure *Use external storage* is *not* activated. 

![Preferences](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/cryptonite-en/27.png)[Cryptonite img 06] ![Preferences](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/cryptonite-en/28.png)[Cryptonite img 07]

*Figure 6 and 7: Preferences*
