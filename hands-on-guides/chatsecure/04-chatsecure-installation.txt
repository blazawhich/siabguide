# Install ChatSecure

## Step 1

**Download** the app from the [Google play store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=info.guardianproject.otr.app.im)


![Gibberbot in the Google Play store](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/gibberbot-and-02.png)

*Figure 1: Gibberbot in the Google Play store*

## Step 2

**Confirm** the permissions required by the app and install the app by tapping the *Install* button


![Permissions and Installation](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/gibberbot-and-03.png) ![](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/gibberbot-and-04.png)

*Figures 2 and 3: Permissions and Installation*

## Step 3

**Tap** *Open* to run the app for the first time.
