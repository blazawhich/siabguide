# Configure ChatSecure

## Step 1

A drop down selection of languages will appear. **Scroll** and **tap** the language you desire for **Gibberbot**.

![Language options](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/gibberbot-and-05.png) 

*Figure 4: Language options*

## Step 2

**Tap** *Get Started* and *Next* 3 times to go through the general information about **Gibberbot**

![Information about Gibberbot](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/gibberbot-and-06.png) ![](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/gibberbot-and-07.png) 

![Information about Gibberbot](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/gibberbot-and-08.png) ![](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/gibberbot-and-09.png) 

*Figures 5, 6, 7, and 8: Information about Gibberbot*

## Step 3

An account configuration page is shown:


![Account configuration page](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/gibberbot-and-10.png) 

*Figure 9: Account configuration page*

## Step 4

**Add** *IM* *user* and *server* data (for example "Joe@gmail.com"), and then **add** your associated password.

![Add account details](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/gibberbot-and-11.png) 

*Figure 10: Add account details*

**Note:** Do **not** check *Remember password*, in case you lose your device.

## Step 5

If you have installed and switched on [**Orbot**](/en/Orbot_main), **check** *Connect using Orbot* for anonymity

![Connect via Tor](/sites/siabnext.ttc.io/files/media/gibberbot-and-12.png) 

*Figure 11: Connect via Tor option*

## Step 6

**Tap** the *Sign in* button. Once this is done, you are ready to use **Gibberbot**
