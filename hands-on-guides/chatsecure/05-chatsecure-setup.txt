# Configure ChatSecure

## Step 1

A drop down selection of languages will appear. **Scroll** and **tap** the language you desire for **Gibberbot**.

![Language options](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/gibberbot-en/05.png)[Chatsecure img 04]

*Figure 4: Language options*

## Step 2

**Tap** *Get Started* and *Next* 3 times to go through the general information about **Gibberbot**

![Information about Gibberbot](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/gibberbot-en/06.png)[Chatsecure img 05] ![Information about Gibberbot](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/gibberbot-en/07.png)[Chatsecure img 06]

![Information about Gibberbot](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/gibberbot-en/08.png)[Chatsecure img 07] ![](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/gibberbot-en/09.png)[Chatsecure img 08]

*Figures 5, 6, 7, and 8: Information about Gibberbot*

## Step 3

An account configuration page is shown:


![Account configuration page](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/gibberbot-en/10.png)[Chatsecure img 09]

*Figure 9: Account configuration page*

## Step 4

**Add** *IM* *user* and *server* data (for example "Joe@gmail.com"), and then **add** your associated password.

![Add account details](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/gibberbot-en/11.png)[Chatsecure img 10]

*Figure 10: Add account details*

**Note:** Do **not** check *Remember password*, in case you lose your device.

## Step 5

If you have installed and switched on [**Orbot**](https://securityinabox.org/Orbot_main)[Orbot Main], **check** *Connect using Orbot* for anonymity

![Connect via Tor](https://securityinabox.org/sbox/screen/gibberbot-en/12.png)[Chatsecure img 11]

*Figure 11: Connect via Tor option*

## Step 6

**Tap** the *Sign in* button. Once this is done, you are ready to use **Gibberbot**
